Our Values

Koala is a software application made to simply the process of returning back to office.

Our Motivation

As coronavirus pandemic put as in a position we have never been before, we are forced to embrace new challenges. We come with a solution which is aiming to solve the management behind organizing a day in the office with limited spots.

Intuitive Design

Starting our project

This section offers an overview of the project. It is also meant to help clarify a few essential aspects regarding the project.

  • Date: 19 October 2020

Idea Validation

This is one of the most important milestones. We needed to go through the Customer Discovery process. Our initial focus should be on testing the problem, and then the solution (test our understanding of the customers' problem or need, and only after validating this, determine if the our solution matches this need).

  • Date: 2 November 2020

Lines

A wireframe represents the skeleton/structure of our app. It describe the functionality of the product and the relations between views. It presents the content/features and where these can be found in the app. The product's design is showcased by mockups, which represent the next stage after wireframes.

  • Date: 9 November 2020

Finishings

Perform at least 3 face-to-face interviews with potential users (from your main targeted customer segment) Create a user persona (or the main user persona in case of targeting multiple customer segments)

To create the user persona shown, we discussed with more people who now work from home. The result is shown in form of Mihai, the manager. We will design the future of this project to suit best his needs, as he being one of our primary customer.

User Stories

From the idea validation and the meetings we had we discovered the main needs of our user persona and created the user stories using a kibana board.

To create the user stories and track them we chose asana - web application build to help any team to organize, follow and manage its work.
